Sprouts

The recipe to this famous Indian breakfast

Sprouts is an Indian breakfast which is made up on primarily Sprouts. It can be made in 10 minutes. But it takes less than 5 minutes to finish.

Ingredients

  • Sprouts
  • Peanuts
  • Onions
  • chilli
  • tomato
  • potato
  • Oil
  • mustard seeds
  • cumin
  • chilli powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the vegetables finely and keep them aside
  2. Soak the sprouts in water overnight. Rinse the water in the morning, keep this aside
  3. Take 1 teaspoon of oil in a pan
  4. Heat the oil in low flame
  5. Put the peanuts in it until they become crispy
  6. Take the peanuts out
  7. Put mustard seeds and cumin
  8. Put the chilli, cook for 1 minute
  9. Put the chopped onions, mix it well and leave till they become translucent
  10. Put other vegetables too, wait for them to cook
  11. Add the spices and cook them for two minutes
  12. Add Sprouts and mix it well
  13. It is ready to serve
